Welcome to Open Mapping

Mecklenburg County believes in the power of open data and open source software to inspire innovation and ingenuity.

Data

Open data:

  • is accessible to everyone
  • is available in open, non-proprietary formats
  • gives you the right to redistribute the data
  • gives you the right to modify and make derivative data and works

Grab some open data today and make something amazing!

Apps

Open source software gives you the freedom to:

  • run the program for any purpose
  • study how the program works and to change it so it does computing as you wish
  • redistribute copies so you can help your neighbor
  • distribute copies of your modified versions to others, giving the community a chance to benefit from your changes

Pull requests are welcome!
